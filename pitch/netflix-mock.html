netflix, uninterrupted

Ads that never touch the story.

Netflix India runs zero ads today. Every plan from ₹149 to ₹649 is ad free, while the global ad tier just crossed 250 million monthly viewers and is heading for three billion dollars this year. So we went looking for the quiet moments inside the app. The pauses, the countdowns, the progress bars. We found fifteen of them, across mobile, TV and desktop, and designed a placement for each one.

one · we never stop the story
two · we never touch the hero
three · we only borrow attention that already exists

nothing to lose

India's most loved ad free app can stay feeling ad free and still earn like an ad platform.
