01 · The idea

One app for everything happening around you, made social.

Today, finding something good to do is scattered. Reviews live on one app, events on another, and your friends' real opinions live in group chats that scroll away. Adda pulls it into one beautiful place. You open it, the map lights up around you, and you instantly see where your friends eat, what they love, and what is on tonight.

The name carries it. An adda is your hangout, your go to spot, a word everyone in Vizag already uses. So the product needs no explanation. "Add it to my addas." "That is our adda." It is talkable by design, and that is how it spreads.

01

Alive, not a directory

Friends' review cards hang on a live map, so the city looks full of real opinions instead of dead grey pins.

02

Ranked, not starred

After a visit we ask, better or worse than the last one, and build your personal ranked list. Addictive, and far richer than five stars.

03

A profile you love

My addas is your map, your ranked lists, your badges and streaks. Good enough that people open the app just to look at themselves.

04

Everything social, one app

Food, places, events, friends and guides together. If someone wants something exciting to do, they come to us first.

The feeling we are chasing

Premium and buttery like Apple Maps and Instagram, and addictive like the best learning apps. Polished enough to trust, playful enough to open every single day. That combination is exactly what the great social apps get right.

04 · How we build it

Big vision, shipped in beautiful stages.

The goal is the full experience. The method is staged, so what we have is always polished and shippable, and we never stall in a half built app.

1Onboarding and foundation. The first impression, interactive and premium from the first tap.
2The live map. Hanging review cards and rich place detail pages.
3Add a spot and rank it. The addictive better or worse comparison flow.
4The profile. My addas, ranked lists, badges and streaks.
5Friend graph. Contacts, usernames, follow and unfollow, friends recommend this.
6Events and guides. What is on now, and curated lists worth following.
7Seeding pipeline. Fill Vizag with real, high quality spots before launch.
