Concept pitch · Netflix India · July 2026

Ads that never touch the story.
Every screen Netflix lives on.

Netflix India runs zero ads today (₹149–₹649/mo, all ad-free) while the global ad tier reaches 250M monthly viewers and ~$3B in 2026 revenue. Fifteen placements across mobile, TV and desktop — all in dead time or browse surfaces. No playback is ever interrupted, and the hero stays editorial.

Rule 1 — never stop the story
Rule 2 — never touch the hero
Rule 3 — only monetize attention that already exists
